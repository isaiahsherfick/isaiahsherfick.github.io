Streamlining The Party Scene

It's happened to the best of us, hasn't it? You're planning an absolute banger of a party, everyone's gonna be there, it's going to be amazing. You've got everything in order: the drinks, the food, the activities... and then it dawns on you: you forgot to hire a DJ. Now you have two days to hire a good DJ who's in your price range. What are you going to do?

Introducing DJFindr

DJFindr is the revolutionary new application from I-101Group1 inc. Inspired by "swipe" applications like Tinder, DJFindr can quickly pair you with the right DJ. No more navigating novice Wordpress sites, Facebook pages, and Twitter feeds trying to figure out how to hire somebody; just swipe right on their DJ profile on DJFindr and they'll be notified that you'd like to hire them! After receiving a right swipe, a DJ has two days to get back to you, or we disable ads in the app for a week at no cost to you!
